Statistics

One of the best ways to help save more lives is by encouraging your friends and family. Start by sharing your special link with them. Then join a team or even start your own team. The more points your team earns, the closer we come to saving more lives.

On this page you will find access to statistics for individuals and teams who have joined together to earn points and compete with other teams.

How to earn points

  1. You earn points by becoming a champion
  2. You earn points from the scores of the quizes you take
  3. You earn points when friends and family follow your link to complete any activity on the site
  4. Individual points sum up to become team points
